Mentors Booking Page

A Website for Booking Professionals

A simple Responsive web app for booking mentors, gives young professionals the chance to get guidance and direction from industry leaders. It also gives experienced professionals the platform to share their experience and impact the upcoming professionals in their fields.

This project was inspired by my quest to book for mentors in the field of software development, I found out that ADPlist is already doing something similar for UX Designers, I was fascinateed by the concept and felt a wider reach would be great.

When I started this project, I had some issues working with reusable components, it took me quite some time to wrap my head around it and one of the skills i gained from this project is knowing when to use reusable components in my designs.
