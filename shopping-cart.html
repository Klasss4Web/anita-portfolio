Simple Shopping Cart

Shopping Cart Built With React

A recruiter asked me to build a simple shopping cart with react in less than three hours. Quite a limited amount of time to get it done, but I managed to get a working shopping cart considering the time available for the task.

This app was built with react under a short period of time. Alot could have been done but, I felt I should keep it simple. No complexity, no react router, just plain react.

My biggest challenge and mistake was keeping react router out of this project, it could have saved me alot of troubles and wasted time. Also, keeping the entire refactoring till the end of the project was another big mistake I made, because I ended up with a functional app and a messed up code base. It was a lesson learnt, one that increased my experience on best practices.
